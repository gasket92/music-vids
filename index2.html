Music Video Collection 2

The Crosstown Rivalry - Closer And Closure

Comment: Pop-punk. Kinda similar to City Lights from the first website!

The Wonder Years - Teenage Parents

Comment: The Wonder Years started as an easycore band but have transitioned into rock/pop.

In Her Own Words - Brand New Me

Comment: This band evolved from easycore to pop rock over time.

These Hearts - Been Through Hell

Comment: Possibly the best easycore album there is. Great breakdown from 2:00-2:30.

Crunkasaurus Rex - Venture

Comment: Easycore but weird! A highly unique band.

Attack Attack! - Stick Stickly

Comment: Metalcore/Post-Hardcore. This band was often associated with "crabcore."

Amaranthe - Trinity

Comment: Pop-metal/power metal with a lot of synths.

Avenged Sevenfold - Blinded in Chains

Comment: Drumming at 02:50 is a standout moment.

Miss May I - A Dance With Aera Cura

Comment: A metalcore classic with a good blend of clean and harsh vocals.

Kublai Khan - Smoke and Mirrors

Comment: Hardcore Metal. More shouting than screaming.

Boundaries - Realize and Rebuild

Comment: Overlapping vocals at the end are a highlight.

All That Remains - This Darkened Heart

Comment: My favorite album when I was around your age.

Sylosis - Teras

Comment: Thrash/Melodic Death Metal. One of my favorite outro riffs.

Anterior - Tyranny

Comment: Amazing solos, but took time for me to appreciate the vocals.

Within The Ruins - Tractor Pull

Comment: Probably metalcore but quite unique. One of the best outros in metal.
