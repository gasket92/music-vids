Music Videos Part 2

The Crosstown Rivalry - Closer And Closure

Pop-punk. Kinda similar to City Lights from the first website!

The Wonder Years - Teenage Parents

The Wonder Years started as an easycore band but the singer is pretty much ashamed of their old music...

In Her Own Words - Brand New Me

This band started off easycore, this whole EP is fantastic...

These Hearts - Been Through Hell

Possibly the best easycore album there is. Great breakdown from 2:00-2:30

Crunkasaurus Rex - Venture

Easycore but weird! These guys are so highly regarded for being super unique...

Attack Attack! - Stick Stickly

Metalcore/Post-Hardcore. Attack Attack got a lot of hate but I thought this album was great...

Amaranthe - Trinity

Female clean singer, male screaming. Not sure what genre you'd put this in...

Avenged Sevenfold - Blinded in Chains

This dude has a pretty unusual voice. Drumming at 02:50 I used to have on repeat!

Miss May I - A Dance With Aera Cura

Very solid song and this album is a metalcore classic. Good blend of clean and harsh vocals.

Kublai Khan - Smoke and Mirrors

Hardcore Metal. I don't really like this genre too much but you should have a check.

Boundaries - Realize and Rebuild

The harsh vocals are a bit hardcorey but I do love this song...

All That Remains - This Darkened Heart

My favourite album when I was around your age...

Sylosis - Teras

Thrash/Melodic Death Metal. One of my favourite outro riffs...

Anterior - Tyranny

Thrash/Melodic Death Metal. Their solos are amazing...

Within The Ruins - Tractor Pull

Probably metalcore still but quite different. These guys have a very distinctive sound...
